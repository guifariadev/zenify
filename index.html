Frequently asked questions

What is Zenify? ▼

Zenify is a meditation app designed to bring calm and mindfulness into your daily routine. Whether you're looking to reduce stress, improve focus, or simply find a moment of peace, Zenify offers guided meditation sessions, soothing nature sounds, and progress tracking to help you on your journey.

How does Zenify help with meditation?▼

Zenify provides a variety of meditation exercises, from beginner-friendly sessions to more advanced practices. Our personalized sessions can be tailored to your goals, such as relaxation, better sleep, or improved concentration. Additionally, Zenify tracks your progress, encouraging consistency and growth.

Can I use Zenify if I'm new to meditation?▼

Absolutely! Zenify is perfect for beginners. Our app offers simple, easy-to-follow guided meditations that introduce you to the practice step-by-step. You can start with short sessions and gradually build up as you become more comfortable with meditation.

Is Zenify free to use? ▼

Yes, Zenify offers a free version that includes access to a selection of meditations and sounds. For users looking for an enhanced experience, we also offer a Premium subscription, which unlocks unlimited meditations, exclusive content, and additional features.

What is included in the Zenify Premium plan? ▼

With Zenify Premium, you get unlimited access to all meditation sessions, exclusive nature soundscapes, personalized progress tracking, and new content added regularly. You can enjoy a 7-day free trial to experience the benefits of Premium before committing.

How do I track my meditation progress? ▼

Zenify tracks your daily meditation sessions, allowing you to see how often you meditate and your overall progress. You can set goals and check your streaks, helping you stay motivated and committed to your practice.
